How to run:
Run in an GameBoy emualtor or on the real thing

should run on a real GameBoy too.
thanks to "MoRpHiUs X" <phed_x69@hotmail.com> for testing

Controlls:
Arrows will move the pointer.
A will sweep the current position
Hold down B and then press A to mark the current position
SELECT will show the setup menu
